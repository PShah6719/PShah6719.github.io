Make Eggs Over Easy

Eggs over easy is an extremely quick and simple way to cook your eggs. All you need are some eggs and a frying pan.

Here's how you can break those eggs and create the perfect omelette.

  1. Heat the frying pan on medium heat.
  2. Once the pan is hot enough, add a teaspoon of oil. Make sure you spread the oil evenly on the surface of the pan.
  3. Crack the eggs open and add them directly to the pan.
  4. Let the eggs cook slowly on medium or low heat. Make sure they are not sticking to the pan.
  5. Once the egg whites have set and turned opaque, carefully flip the omelette over.
  6. Let it cook for 5-6 seconds, then carefully remove the omelette from the pan.
